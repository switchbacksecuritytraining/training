Abstract

This course gives you the knowledge, skills and hands-on experience needed to respond to security incidents in AWS environments.

Across a mix of lectures, labs and Capture The Flag challenges, you'll learn how to detect, analyse, contain and recover from cloud-based compromises within AWS. We provide access to the cloud tenants and the investigation data, so you work the incidents on real ground rather than on slides.

What you'll
be able to do

AWS attacks

A deep understanding of the attacks that land in AWS, and how to respond to them.

Forensic analysis

Hands-on experience performing forensic analysis of the relevant AWS logging.

Incident triage

The ability to perform incident triage in AWS environments.
